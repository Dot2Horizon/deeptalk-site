Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

DeepTalk is a privacy-first app. All chat analysis is performed 100% on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your chat data to any external server.

1. Data We Do NOT Collect

DeepTalk does not collect any of the following:

  • Chat messages, conversation history, or message content
  • Contact information or address book data
  • Photos, media files, or attachments
  • Location or GPS data
  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV)
  • Browsing history or search queries

2. Data We Collect

We collect minimal, anonymous data solely to improve app quality:

Data Type Purpose Linked to You?
Usage Analytics App improvement No (anonymous)
Crash Reports Bug fixing No (anonymous)

3. Analytics Services

TelemetryDeck — We use TelemetryDeck for anonymous usage analytics. TelemetryDeck is designed to be privacy-first and does not use cookies or collect personal data. Events tracked include: app launches, feature usage (screen visits), and import success/failure rates. No personal identifiers are included.

TelemetryDeck Privacy Policy: telemetrydeck.com/privacy

Firebase Crashlytics — We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports. These reports include device model, OS version, and crash stack traces. No personal data or chat content is included.

Firebase Privacy Policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4. On-Device Processing

All analysis features in DeepTalk run entirely on your device:

  • Chat Parsing — Message files are parsed locally
  • Analytics — All 10 analytics cards compute on-device
  • AI Chat Insight — Powered by an on-device LLM (large language model) that runs locally using your device's processor
  • Wrapped — Summary reports are generated on-device
  • Semantic Search — Vector embeddings are computed and stored locally

Your chat data never leaves your iPhone. There are no cloud APIs, no server-side processing, and no data uploads.

5. Data Storage

Imported chat data is stored in the app's sandboxed container on your device:

  • Protected by iOS file-level encryption at rest
  • Accessible only by the DeepTalk app
  • Completely deleted when you uninstall the app
  • You can delete individual analyses at any time within the app

6. Network Usage

DeepTalk's network access is strictly limited to:

  • Sending anonymous usage analytics to TelemetryDeck
  • Sending crash reports to Firebase Crashlytics

No chat data, personal information, or analysis results are ever transmitted over the network.

7. Third-Party SDKs

SDK Purpose Data Shared
TelemetryDeck Anonymous analytics Usage events only
Firebase Crashlytics Crash reporting Crash logs only

Neither service receives any chat data, personal information, or analysis results.

8. Children's Privacy

DeepTalk does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. The app does not require account creation or collect any personal identifiers.

9. Your Rights

Since DeepTalk does not collect personal data:

  • There is no personal data to access, correct, or delete on our servers
  • You can delete all local data by uninstalling the app
  • You can delete individual analyses within the app at any time

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
